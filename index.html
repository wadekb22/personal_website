What I do

I'm a problem-solver at heart

It's become a habit of mine to always question the ways each thing works and wonder how it could be better executed. Inevitably, a nagging sense stirs whenever I see inefficiency. Surrounded by potential improvement, I can't help but think how I can fix them. It's an intimidating mission, but it's also what drives me to create and learn.


I'll never finish learning

The more I know, the better equipped I am to face the world's challenges. There's more knowledge in the world than anyone can wield. Perhaps with data science, I might be able to use a fraction of it to change the world.
